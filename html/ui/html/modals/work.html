Information Regarding the Proof-Of-Work Fund
The proof-of-work fund is used to periodically pay the workers. In your setup, XEL are put in the proof-of-work fund. The higher this amount is, the longer your job will stay live. After the proof-of-work fund is empty, the job is closed and-if applicable-the bounty fund is refunded.
Information Regarding the Bounty Fund
The bounty fund is split among all bounty submissions after the job has been closed. In your current setup, XEL are put in the bounty fund. Please note, this amount can only be refunded if no bounties have been submitted. The first bounty submission consumes the entire amount.
